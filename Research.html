AI & Coding Club

The Research Club provides students with an opportunity to explore academic research beyond the classroom. Members collaborate on independent and group research projects across a range of disciplines, developing skills in critical thinking, academic writing, data analysis, and presentation. The club supports students interested in competitions, conferences, and future university research by offering mentorship, peer feedback, and structured guidance throughout the research process.

About the Club

The AI & Coding Club is a community of students passionate about programming, artificial intelligence, and creating real-world tech projects. We welcome all skill levels — beginners to advanced.
