Public Speaking Club

Helping students gain confidence, communication skills, and leadership abilities.

About the Club

The Public Speaking Club is dedicated to helping students become confident, expressive, and persuasive speakers. Whether you're preparing for debates, presentations, or just improving your everyday communication, this club provides the tools and environment to grow.

Club Information

  • Meeting Day: Monday
  • Time: During break
  • Location: Small ground
  • Club Leader: Ahmed Usman Butt , Selim eymen anil and Ali Al Bachari

What We Do

  • Prepare weekly speeches and presentations
  • Host structured and impromptu debates
  • Learn voice projection, tone control, and stage presence
  • Guest speaker sessions from professionals
  • Participate in school-wide speaking competitions
